Spirited Away

This is the most famous Japanese animeted movie

What is this animated movie about?: The main character name is Chihiro, she is 10-years old, is on her way to a new house with her parents. However, she is worry about the new life. Before long, the car they are in stumbles into a mysterious town. Her parents are grawn to the strangeness of the town and continue to venture further into it. However, because they broke the rules of the town, her parents are turned into pigs...

The main characters

Chihiro and Haku

Who is Haku?: In fact, Haku's true form was the god of the "Kohaku River" where chihiro nearly drowned when she was child. After remembering his true name, Haku turns back from his white gragon form into a human form.
